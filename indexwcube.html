Welcome Trumpet Connoisseurs

Our unique trumpet store is brought to you buy Chuck Berger, a novice and student of Crossfire's Building Advanced Websites Course. With no programming experience, he tried to keep up week after week with his gifted and talented classmates. Alas, this website is what he mustered from the inifinite subject matter and information provided by the class curriculum. He found a design template that appealed to him, used it as a foundation, and changed it using techniques he retained. While Chuck likes guitars and would love to learn to play, he chose to create a trumpet shop since the teachers agreed choosing something other than guitars would be acceptable.
